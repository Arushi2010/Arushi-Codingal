My School

My name is Arushi Paul, and I am a student of Class 10 A at GD Goenka Signature School, Sohna, Gurugram. My school is not one of the best in the area, with a huge campus and amazing facilities. We have modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, and even an indoor sports complex! The teachers here are really supportive and make learning interesting. Apart from academics, the school focuses on extracurricular activities like music, dance, and sports, which help us grow in all aspects. I feel lucky to be part of such a great institution that prepares us for the future while making learning fun.
